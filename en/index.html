Services

Creating tangible value with software

Functioning software with rapid benefits and short innovation cycles is our passion. Living agility instead of celebrating it. We offer our experience in the entire lifecycle of software product development as services services:

Consulting

Doing the right things. With today’s unlimited possibilities, this is one of the biggest challenges:

  • CTO as a Service + Team Crew
  • Status quo analysis, architecture review and roadmap conception
  • Individual solution finding with the use of standard technologies
Development

Product development is not just coding. It is a holistic concept and passion:

  • Development of technology and product concepts
  • Realization and implementation
  • Project management and responsibility
Operation

The decisive phase of your product is the operational phase, in which it brings the features to its users and generates feedback:

  • Iterations with the product as a further development
  • Monitoring and optimization
  • Rollout and operation
